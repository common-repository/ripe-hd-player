= How difficult to use this plug-in in wordpress? =

Wordpress already made all plug-in usability as simple as possible. Our video player plug-in is even easier to work.  This plug-in is used to showcase your HD videos on your wordpress blog or websites. Video player used in this plug-in is developed in Adobe Flash Action Script 3. This player enables the high definition video streaming in your blog or website. How to use documentation also provided along with the plug-in installation. You will be able to see the documentation tab under the plug-in which can guide you to setup the video players on your wordpress.

It is true, this plug-in is really very simple to use. Upload your videos and use URLs in plug-in to integrate the video content to the Ripe HD flv player. In addition to video URL, you will also have video player settings in the configuration pages which can help you to configure the player elements (like player controls, sidebar) as you like.

It is guaranteed that you or your visitor will not wait for minutes to load the player and see the video. It loads quickly in milliseconds. The entire flash video player application files size is less than 40KB. No more waiting and you better start experiencing it.

== Installation ==

= Basic Installation =
1.	Download and upload the zip file in wordpress plugin installation page or unzip and upload it manually to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory
1.	Activate the Ripe HD Player plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress and that's all.


= This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working. =

1. After finishing the basic installation, you will have 'Ripe HD Player' Plugin in your plugin list.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Ripe HD Player plugin will be shown in the your tool bar
1. Navigate to Ripe HD Player tab and further more instruction will be there to help you.
